    Bloodbank Management System

    The Blood Bank Management System is a software solution designed to efficiently manage the inventory of blood units, donor information, and blood donation processes within a blood bank or blood donation center

    Donor Management: Registration of blood donors with their personal details such as name, contact information, blood type, medical history, and donation history.

    Inventory Management: Tracking and management of blood inventory including blood units, blood types, expiry dates, and storage conditions. Real-time monitoring of blood stock levels to ensure an adequate supply of blood for emergencies.

    Blood Collection: Recording details of blood collection including donor information, collection date, and blood type. Generation of unique identifiers or barcodes for each blood unit to track its journey from donation to distribution.

    Distribution and Allocation: Efficient allocation of blood units to hospitals, clinics, or other medical facilities based on their specific requirements and requests. Prioritization of blood distribution based on factors like blood type, urgency, and location. Tracking the movement of blood units from the blood bank to the recipient facility.

    User Management and Security: Role-based access control to ensure that only authorized personnel can perform certain actions within the system. Secure storage and encryption of sensitive donor and patient information to maintain confidentiality and comply with privacy regulations.
